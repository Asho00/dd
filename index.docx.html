IMC

Integrated medical care

OVERVIEW

It is a system that was invented to help the medical staff, but under their supervision, as it contributed to reducing the burden on doctors in cases of extreme pressure, and developing health care. This system is a medical robot that was created based on the field of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the field of Robotics, as this robot predicts the diagnosis of some diseases, including diabetes and some types of cancer, which helps in early diagnosis, ease of detecting the disease in its early stages, and ease of treatment. It also measures some of the patients’ vital signs, which are body temperature, sugar, and heartbeat. This was done using some medical sensors.  This was aimed at providing medical care to patients in the event of epidemics or cases of infectious disease. It was also used to help people with special needs carry out daily tasks, alert them to the timing of medication, and provide them with health care.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Due to the recent spread of diseases and epidemics, such as Covid-19, which has negatively affected medical care and early diagnosis of diseases, which has led to the disease progressing in its final stages and thus becoming difficult to treat. For some diseases, such as cancer, delay in discovering them causes death, and also causes pressure on the medical worker.  This led to pressure on hospitals and doctors, and they faced the risk of death and fatigue, which led to unintentional human errors. There were some people suffering from difficulty in accessing medical care, whether due to long distances, time constraints, or a lack of health resources in some areas.
